About wORDsana

wORDsana is a web app that allows you to make your one collection of words and sounds.
You can log in, record yourself and listen to other people's collections!

word = ord in Swedish (Svenska), Norwegian (Norsk) and Danish (Dansk)
         = sana in Finnish (Suomi)

Why Swedish, Norwegian, Danish and Finnish?
... Well, "ord" and "sana" look nice and go well together (in my opinion).
